Baked Avocado Fries

Indulge in a tasty and healthier version of fries with these delicious baked avocado slices! All you need is a few simple ingredients and a hot oven to transform ripe avocados into golden, crispy treats. Coated in a combination of flour, eggs, and panko breadcrumbs, these fries are sure to satisfy your snack cravings. Serve them hot out of the oven with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking spray or olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the avocados into 1/2-inch slices.
  3. Place the flour in one shallow dish, the eggs in a second shallow dish, and the breadcrumbs in a third shallow dish. Season the breadcrumbs with the salt and pepper.
  4. Dip each avocado slice into the flour, then the eggs, and then the breadcrumbs, making sure to coat each slice evenly.
  5. Place the coated avocado slices on the prepared baking sheet and lightly spray or brush them with cooking spray or olive oil.
  6. Bake the avocado fries for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve the avocado fries with your choice of dipping sauce, such as ranch dressing, sriracha mayo, or tomato ketchup. Enjoy!
