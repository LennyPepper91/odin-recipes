Simple Quiche

Savory crepes are a delicious and fun twist on traditional pancakes. Imagine a thin, delicate pancake wrapped around your favorite fillings. Maybe you'll stuff yours with sautéed vegetables and gooey cheese, or maybe you'll opt for a spicy filling of chorizo and peppers. Whatever you choose, these crepes are sure to delight your taste buds. The best part? You can enjoy them for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, making them a versatile and convenient meal option. So why wait? Break out the blender and give these savory crepes a try!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup diced cooked bacon (or cooked sausage, ham, or vegetables)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1 cup grated cheese (such as cheddar, mozzarella, or Swiss)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 frozen pie crust (homemade or store-bought)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a small skillet, cook the bacon (or sausage, ham, or vegetables) over medium heat until it is browned and crispy. Remove the bacon from the pan and set it aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook the onions and bell peppers (if using) until they are softened.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Stir in the cooked bacon, onions, bell peppers, and grated cheese.
  5. Place the frozen pie crust in a pie dish or quiche pan. Pour the egg mixture into the crust and smooth the top.
  6. Bake the quiche for 35-45 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the center is set.
  7. Let the quiche cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
