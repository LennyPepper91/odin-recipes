Savory Crepes

Savory crepes are a delicious and fun twist on traditional pancakes. Imagine a thin, delicate pancake wrapped around your favorite fillings. Maybe you'll stuff yours with saut√©ed vegetables and gooey cheese, or maybe you'll opt for a spicy filling of chorizo and peppers. Whatever you choose, these crepes are sure to delight your taste buds. The best part? You can enjoy them for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, making them a versatile and convenient meal option. So why wait? Break out the blender and give these savory crepes a try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the flour, milk, eggs, butter, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Brush the pan with a small amount of butter or oil.
  3. Pour about 1/4 cup of the crepe batter into the pan, tilting the pan to spread the batter evenly. Cook the crepe for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to brown and the crepe is set.
  4. Flip the crepe and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the other side is lightly browned.
  5. Transfer the crepe to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter, brushing the pan with butter or oil as needed.
